DEPARTMENT OF APPAREL DESIGN AND FASHION TECHNOLOGY(Un-Aided)

The program prepares you for an engrossed study of fashion industry covering the entire gamut of processes from textile making, designing, production and marketing. This is desinied to suit creating individuals to excel in the field of the natural talent.

DEPARTMENT HISTORY AND FACILITIES

The department was established in the year 2009 with a sanctioned intake of 30 students.In the course of time the department grew in strength with sufficient faculty members and sufficient technical members. The students intake also has increased.The department contributes in enhaving the prime concepts of fashion technology and apparel design,pattern marking, garment construction,surface ornamentation like hand embroidery,machine embroidery,fabric painting, knitting fashion merchandising, textile designing and testing. The industry internship provides the prospective designer to position themselves in the apparel fields.

OUR FACULTY

The department has well experienced faculty members supported by technical staff and non-technical staff members. The faculty members have wide experience in teaching and delivering practical knowledge to the students.The commitment and expertise of the faculty has enhanced the competance level of our students helping the students seeking employment are prepared to meet the demands of the industry. This is a job oriented course aiming to train the students in the field of fashion designing, pattern making , grament constructions.

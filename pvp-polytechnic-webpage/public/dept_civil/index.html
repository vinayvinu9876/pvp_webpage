WELCOME TO THE DEPARTMENT OF CIVIL ENGINEERING

DEPARTMENT OF CIVIL ENGINEERING

Civil engineers create, improve and protect the environment in which we live .They plan,design and oversee construction and maintenance of building structurs and infeastructure , such as roads, railways ,airports ,bridges, harbours,dams,irrigation projects,power plants,and water and sewerage systems.

Quality Education

The quality education is one that is pedagogically and develpomentally sound and educates the student in becoming an active & productive members of society. it contribute to peaceful and democratic societies and enhance individual well-being.

SYSTEMATIC APPROACH

In the context of education,system is a unit as a whole incorporating all its aspects and parts,namely,pupils,teachers,curriculum,context and evaluation of instructional objectives.The teaching,learning process is viewed as communication and control taking place between the components of a system.

ONE TO ONE STUDY

One to one teaching is made special by the fact that many techers have to develop there own strategies, approaches and materials. This approaches and techniques to help effective learning

MISSION

1.To offer Technical programme in civil engineering and other skill development courses that add value to the student competencies. 2.To promote quality education,research and consultancy for construction industry and socciety needs. 3.To inculcate moral and ethical values among the students. 4.To encourage students to pursue higher eduaction and take competetive examinations in various carrier enhancing courses.

VISION

To become a institution of excellence that brings out the civil engineers with high technical competencies and to promote high end knowledge to meet the current and future challenges in civil engineering field and also to create a sustainable environment to enhance the global quality of life.

DEPARTMENT HISTORY AND FACILITIES

The college and department came under the grant-in-aid scheme,Government of karnataka in the year 2001.
The Civil Department is one of the oldest Department established in the year 1987.
It has well established lab facilities like
  1. Material construction lab
  2. Material testing lab
  3. Hydraulics and environmental lab
  4. Surveying lab with advanced Surveying equippments
  5. Computer lab.

The lab facilities are full-fledged as per AICTE norms with latest instruments such as Total stations, Electronic theodolite, Autolevel, Dumpy levels etc.The materials testing lab is well established to conduct every experiments prescribed by the Directorate of Technical Education. The Computer lab is also established with licensed version softwares.

OUR FACULTY

The Department has Nine faculty memebers supported by a instructor and an assistant instructor with three mechanics and four helpers.The faculty memebers have wide experience in teaching as well as good rapport with the construction industry. The staff are dedicated to perform their duties effeciently,effectively and ethically at individual level and also at group level in a multidisciplinary team.Thus contributing to the overall development of the student community,to analyse data and technical concepts pertaining to the development of infrastructure design,sustainability,construction management and other related fields of civil enginerring. The non-technical staffs are well trained to assist the students during practical sessions.

DEPARTMENT HISTORY AND FACILITIES

The department was established in the year 1992 with a intake of 40 students .shri N.B.Umashankar was the first faculty member appointed in the department. The college and department came under the grant-in-aid scheme,Government of karnataka in the year 2001.In due course of time the department grew in strength and presently we are three dynamic faculty members and three-non teaching staff members.The students intake also increased to 60.The labs are well-equipped to meet the students requirement .The computer lab has 18 systems with LAN facilities,with backup of inverter.our department students have secured the second and third rank in the BTE diploma examination held in april/may-2002 and april/may-2004 respectively. The department has Basic Electrical Engineering and Electronics components lab, Semi conductor devices lab,Digital electronics laboratory,Analog circuits lab,Electronic measurement lab.The Biomedical instrumentation laboratory like ECG machine,Electronic BP operatus,sphygmomanometer,Hemoglobin meter.The industrial Instrumentation lab is fully equipped.Many simple equipments/modules have been designed and constructed by our students for Bio Medical Labs and Control System Lab,under the able guidance of the faculty members.

OUR FACULTY

The department has three faculty members supported by three non-teaching staff members the electronics and Communication faculties share the work load.The faculty members have wide experience in teaching and delivering practical knowledge to the students.The commitments and expertise of the faculty has enhance to the competance level of our students helping them to secure admission in prestigious institutions to pursue higher education.The non-teaching staff are well trained to assist the students during practical sessions.

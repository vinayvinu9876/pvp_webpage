About Us

P.V.P Polytechnic was established in the year 1987-88 by Panchajanya Vidyapheeta Welfare Trust (PVPWT) Bangalore. PVPWT is founded by Late Sri. M. H. Jayaprakash Narayan. The Institution was started with approved by A.I.C.T.E New Delhi and aided by government of Karnataka in Departments of Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE). Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Information Science and Engineering , Instrumentation Technology and Apparel design and fashion technology(Un-aided)

The Institution is aided by Government of Karnataka, approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and is affiliated to B.T.E Bangalore.

The P.V.P Polytechnic has its own four storeyed building sprawling campus in the premises of Dr.Ambedkar institute of technology on the outer ring road,adjacent to jnana Bharathi campus of Bangalore University just 10km from heart of Bangalore city.

The campus is well connected to all parts of the city by our own institute buses and Bangalore Metropolitan Transport Corporation(BMTC)buses.

As a grant-in-aid institution of Government of Karnataka,100% of seats are filled up purely by merit by the department of technical education.

The Institution has a sufficient built-up area. with a large number of spacious, well ventilated class rooms, smart class rooms with e-learning facilities, Laboratories, Data Center, Workshops, Drawing Halls, Seminar Halls, Library and Information Center, Sports Complex, and Auditorium.

The Institution is having well qualified faculty members involved in various research, testing and consultancy activities and carrying out technical works of practical importance. This has resulted in publications in good refereed journals and patents.
